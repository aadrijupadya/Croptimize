Our Mission

Due to climate change, crop yield and output has become unpredictable around the world

Our application helps farmers by providing insights on weather, crop production, and natural disasters (namely wildfires), in order to make efficient use of available resources and estimate quantities such as crop yield and preciptiation.

Statistics

$12.5 Billion in Crop and Forage Losses due to 2021 California Forest Fires and other natural disasters

Due to a variety off actors such as natural disasters, unpredictable weather, and lack of planning, crop production can severely fall and have drastic affects on both the lives of farmers, and the economy as a whole.

Our Tools and Processes

We make use of a variety of tools and processes to assist farmers

Ensemble ML models for crop production

We utilize regression models that were trained on historical USDA data to provide insight about crop production, given a specific location or crop

Ensemble ML models for weather prediction

Using a regression model trained on historical NOAA weather data, we try to provide monthly predictions for weather based on location, and ideal crops to grow based on this weather.

Forest Fire Alerts

Utilizing web scraping and a custom API, we check for forest fires hourly and provide alerts if these fires are close to the farmer's location.
